The Origin

This server was originally created on 18th May,2021 during the covid lockdown when two buddies (D&Kylos) out of boredom started going to morning walks.As days turned into months, the morning walks converted more into morning watches when one day, D discovered the Origin, in its full pride and shine. The buds got a motivation from the Origin and they started simping for it when one morning, the Origin disappeared.It never came back again. D&Kylos out of rememberance made a minecraft server-The Origin where they used to share all their joy and sorrows in form of gameplays.Since then,many other heroes have been added to this SMP,many departed, different lures have taken place but the motive is always constant-
